On AI & This Site

This website was built with AI as a learning project. I believe AI is a powerful tool, and I selectively use it to assist with writeups and as a way to learn. Everything I post here has had hours of work and research poured into it — I will never copy and paste AI output directly onto this site. The goal of CyberSneedHQ is to share knowledge in my own words, as I actually experienced it. My CTF challenges and their writeups are kept intentionally raw, with no AI involved.
