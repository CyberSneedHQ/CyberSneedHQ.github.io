Hello World

Howdy, I'm Hunter Sneed, the Cyber Space Cowboy.

I ride two trails and enjoy them both. One is cybersecurity operations: threat hunting, incident response, and digital forensics. Chasing down what happened, how, and who. The other is engineering the machinery that makes that work possible: SIEM design, detection pipelines, and network visibility infrastructure. Building the thing, not just running it.

This outpost is where I keep what I'm working on: writeups, projects, CTFs, and whatever I'm currently taking apart. Whether you're a fellow defender, a recruiter, or just passing through, welcome to the HQ.

Free work for those who need it. If you're an under-resourced outfit, a nonprofit, a small local org, anyone who needs real detection capability or incident response help and can't pay for it, reach out. I'll get you back on your feet, drive the outlaws out, and work to prevent the situation from happening in the future. build your SIEM or logging pipeline at no cost. The folks getting hit hardest are usually the ones who can least afford a defense.

On AI & This Site

This website was built with the assistance of AI as a learning project. I believe AI is a powerful tool, and I selectively use it to assist with writeups and as a way to learn. Everything I post here has had hours of work and research poured into it — I will never copy and paste AI output directly onto this site. The goal of CyberSneedHQ is to share knowledge in my own words, as I actually experienced it. My CTF challenges and their writeups are kept intentionally raw, with no AI involved.
