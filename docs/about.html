About

Howdy — I'm Hunter Sneed, the Cyber Space Cowboy.

I ride two trails and enjoy them both. One is cybersecurity operations — threat hunting, incident response, and digital forensics. Chasing down what happened, how, and who. The other is engineering the machinery that makes that work possible — SIEM design, detection pipelines, and network visibility infrastructure. Building the thing, not just running it.

This outpost is where I post what I'm working on: writeups, projects, CTFs, and whatever I'm currently taking apart.

Free work for those who need it. If you're an under-resourced outfit — a nonprofit, a small local org, anyone who needs real detection capability and can't pay for it — reach out. I'll design and build your SIEM or logging pipeline at no cost. The folks getting hit hardest are usually the ones who can least afford a defense.
